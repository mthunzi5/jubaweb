About Us

Who We Are

Juba Skills Development Academy (Pty) Ltd trading as KZN Project Management is a professional training and development company offering accredited training, consultancy, and project management services across South Africa.

We are accredited with various Sector Education and Training Authorities (SETAs) and have extensive experience managing large-scale training projects, ensuring full compliance with SETA and QCTO standards.

Comprehensive Skills Development Services

At Juba Skills Development Academy, we provide end-to-end skills development solutions tailored to your organization's needs. Our comprehensive suite of services ensures compliance, maximizes SETA funding opportunities, and drives meaningful workforce transformation.

Strategic Planning & Compliance

Workplace Skills Plan (WSP) Compilation & Submission

We compile comprehensive annual Workplace Skills Plans for SETA submission based on your company's strategic objectives, workplace needs, and employee personal records. Our WSPs are designed to address critical scarce skills needs in your industry while ensuring alignment with Employment Equity (EE) and Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment (BBBEE) requirements.

  • Strategic alignment with business objectives
  • EE and BBBEE integration
  • Industry-specific skills gap analysis
  • SETA compliance guarantee

Annual Training Report (ATR) Completion & Submission

Accurate and timely ATR submissions to demonstrate training implementation and unlock mandatory grant benefits. We ensure your organization meets all SETA reporting requirements while maximizing grant recovery opportunities.

  • Accurate data compilation and verification
  • Mandatory grant optimization
  • Timely submission management

Analysis & Assessment

Training Needs Analysis (TNA) & Skills Audit

We conduct comprehensive quarterly training needs analyses by liaising with line managers to identify skills gaps and training requirements. Our data-driven approach includes research on corporate performance, employee training needs, and career development opportunities to design effective, targeted training programmes.

  • Quarterly skills gap analysis
  • Line manager consultation and buy-in
  • Data-driven training programme design
  • Career development pathway mapping

Assessment & Moderation Services

Professional assessment and moderation services ensuring quality assurance and compliance with QCTO and SETA requirements. Our qualified assessors and moderators maintain the highest standards of evaluation and certification.

  • QCTO-compliant assessment practices
  • External moderation services
  • Quality assurance protocols

Implementation & Management

Project Management Services

End-to-end project management for training initiatives, ensuring successful implementation of workplace skills plans, learnership programmes, and skills development projects. We handle all aspects from planning to reporting.

  • Full project lifecycle management
  • Stakeholder coordination
  • Risk management and mitigation
  • Monthly progress reporting

Learnership Implementation & Management

Complete learnership programme management from registration to completion. We assist companies in establishing and implementing learnerships, ensuring compliance with all SETA requirements while managing learner coaching, assessments, and certification.

  • Learnership programme design
  • SETA registration and compliance
  • Learner recruitment and placement
  • Ongoing coaching and support
  • Assessment coordination

Training Committee Establishment

We assist organizations in establishing effective training committees that drive skills development initiatives and ensure alignment between business strategy and workforce development.

Consulting & Advisory Services

Accreditation Consulting

Expert guidance through the accreditation process for training providers seeking QCTO, SETA, or other regulatory body approvals. We navigate complex requirements to achieve and maintain accreditation status.

Discretionary Grants Advisory

We advise and assist organizations in obtaining discretionary grants from SETAs by identifying funding opportunities, preparing compelling applications, and ensuring compliance with grant conditions. Our expertise maximizes your training investment returns.

  • Grant opportunity identification
  • Application preparation and submission
  • Compliance management
  • Reporting and claim submissions

Tax Incentives Advisory

Guidance on accessing tax incentives for training and employment programmes, including learnership tax allowances and other government incentives that reduce training costs.

Industry Intelligence & Updates

We keep your organization informed about industry-related changes, emerging training opportunities, special SETA grants, and regulatory updates. Stay ahead with our continuous monitoring and advisory services.

Quality Systems & Compliance

Quality Management Systems Development

Development of comprehensive quality management systems including policy development, standard operating procedures, and compliance frameworks. We create robust systems that ensure training quality and regulatory compliance.

Monthly Training Reports

Comprehensive monthly reporting on training activities, learner progress, budget utilization, and compliance status. Our detailed reports provide visibility and support data-driven decision-making.
