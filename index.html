About JUBA CONSULTANTS

JUBA CONSULTANTS is a black female-owned professional consulting company based in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. The company operates as a multidisciplinary enterprise delivering skills development, artificial intelligence innovation, and information and communication technology (ICT) solutions.

JUBA CONSULTANTS is committed to empowering individuals, communities, and organizations through education, technology, and future-focused digital solutions that contribute to socio-economic development and sustainable transformation.
